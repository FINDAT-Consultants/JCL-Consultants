Strategy & Assurance Planning

We help organisations design clear, actionable strategies while embedding confidence and resilience into every stage of planning and execution.

How We Can Help

In today’s complex and dynamic business environment, organisations must do more than react to change — they must anticipate it. Our Strategy & Assurance Planning services provide a structured approach to defining organisational goals, navigating uncertainty, and ensuring that business plans are grounded in robust risk management and performance oversight frameworks.

We combine forward‑thinking strategic advisory with rigorous assurance processes to ensure that organisational objectives are not only well‑conceived but also effectively implemented and monitored. We work with boards, executive leadership teams, and management to develop strategic plans aligned with market realities and future trends, create assurance frameworks that safeguard objectives, strengthen governance structures, identify risks early, and support long‑term stakeholder value creation.

Our Strategy & Assurance Planning Services Include:

  • Strategic Planning Workshops & Facilitation: Defining vision, mission, strategic priorities, and action plans through structured workshops and facilitated leadership sessions.
  • Strategic Risk Identification & Mitigation Planning: Proactively detecting internal and external risks that could affect strategic objectives and crafting mitigation strategies.
  • Governance Framework Design & Implementation: Developing board governance structures, executive oversight models, and assurance reporting systems that drive accountability.
  • Performance Management Frameworks: Designing KPIs, balanced scorecards, and reporting dashboards to track progress and enable data‑driven decisions.
  • Organisational Resilience & Crisis Planning: Building contingency plans, business continuity strategies, and crisis management protocols for operational disruptions.
  • Assurance Reviews & Gap Analysis: Conducting independent reviews of strategic initiatives and assurance structures to highlight gaps, risks, and enhancement opportunities.
  • Stakeholder Engagement Strategy: Creating engagement plans that align internal and external stakeholders around the organisation’s strategic vision and inspire confidence in its delivery.

Our approach is collaborative and adaptive. We work alongside your teams to build capability, transfer knowledge, and foster a culture of strategic thinking and proactive assurance. Across Zambia and the wider region, we help public and private sector organisations unlock opportunities, manage risks, and deliver measurable strategic outcomes.

Why Strategy & Assurance Planning Matters

Organisations today face increasing regulatory scrutiny, rising stakeholder expectations for transparency, accelerated technological disruption, and heightened pressure to demonstrate resilience and sustainability. Without a well‑thought‑out strategy — and without the assurance processes to guide and protect that strategy — organisations risk falling behind or failing altogether.

Our services ensure your organisation is future‑ready, transparent, accountable, and resilient — prepared to respond quickly to crises, provide evidence‑based assurance, and maintain strategic focus in a rapidly changing world.
